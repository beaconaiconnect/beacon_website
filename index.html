Inside the product

See Beacon at work on your accounts.

Accounts

Every account scored, ranked, and routed for today.

Accounts move between active, at risk, cooling, and ready to advance based on live signals, each with a confidence score and the reason attached.

  • Confidence score on every account
  • Ranked by what changed, updated live
  • Risk and cooling flags before it's too late
Contacts

The live buying group, with the signal behind every name.

Beacon finds the people actually in-market, shows their engagement history, and drafts the outreach, so reps reach the right person at the right moment.

  • Full engagement timeline per contact
  • Draft outreach generated in one click
  • Source-backed intent, never a guess
Plays

A task execution hub, not a to-do list.

Plays are predefined actions Beacon runs across your GTM stack. Each shows what it found, what it's about to do, and how many accounts it touched. Run it, preview it, or skip it.

  • Preview before anything runs
  • Executes across your whole stack
  • Impact measured against a holdout
