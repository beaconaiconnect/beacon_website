How Beacon works

A whole back office in your pocket

A market analyst, a media buyer, a stock-watcher and a bookkeeper, working across your systems, in your language, for the price of a subscription.

📡
01 · SENSE

Sees inside and out

Fuses the outside market (competitors, pricing, demand, reviews) with your own systems: sales, payments, inventory, CRM and socials.

🧠
02 · DECIDE

Finds every move worth making

Growth openings, stock running low, lapsed customers, cross-system gaps. Beacon scores it all and surfaces only what's specific and worth acting on.

Runs ads and messages, drafts reorders, tags customer segments, writes back to your CRM and inventory. You approve; Beacon executes.

Reports, remembers, improves

Plain-language results, every action logged and reversible, honest about misses. Beacon gets sharper on your business over time.
